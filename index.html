Proof of Dog is the Future

Of the 471 million pet dogs in the world, fewer than 0.001% have their own bank accounts. That’s not even counting the other 421 million free-range dogs that remain not only unbanked, but also outside the domestication system. There are nearly one billion unbanked dogs.

Dogetag is the solution to this disparity.

When you register your dog on DogeTag, you create a Dogecoin address for your dog. As a responsible dog owner, you want your beloved dog to stay healthy and happy by giving them proper documentation and the best financial tools they can get access to.

Your DogeTag is your proof of dog, and proof of dog is the future.

Oh, and did we mention it’s 100% doge-approved?

Here is how to register:

  1. Enter your Twitter username.
  2. Enter your dog’s name and tap Submit.
  3. Download your QR code.
  4. Tap Press to Tweet.
  5. Upload a photo of your dog with the QR code added to the picture. (Important: You have to add the QR code to the photo. Otherwise, we will not be able to confirm your registration.)
  6. Click Tweet.
  7. Check your inbox. You will receive a DM confirming your dog has been registered.
    8. That’s it.
